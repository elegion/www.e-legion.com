Mobile application Vkarmane

We participated in the development of a mobile product “Vkarmane” for Tinkoff Bank.

Vkarmane keeps and helps to organize the documents important for you and your family. The app enables users to send uploaded docs via email and SMS and reminds you of the date of expiry for your passport or insurance.
