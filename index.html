Full cycle agency

We build apps from scratch or plug into the  process at any stage you want us to.

  • Analyze

    We create specifications and guidelines analyzing the customers’ business flows and users needs to build convenient mobile apps.

  • Make prototypes

    We work on the use cases to develop fleshed-out concepts that will stand for fusion of form, functionality and effectiveness.

  • Design

    We apply the mobile platforms guidelines to the brand identity and make a beautiful and user-friendly app design.

  • Develop

    We create an extensible architecture and write a clean and stable code. We integrate with the technologies of our clients.

  • QA

    We perform manual functional testing and create UI-autotests. We prepare checklists and test cases.

  • Support

    We work under the SLA. We monitor the stability of the application work and make the apps ready for new devices and iOS and Android versions.

Questions

  • Do you give the guarantee for the mobile application?

    Yes, we provide 3-month service guarantee. During this period we will fix all the bugs you find. The main requirement is not to change the source code.

  • What does T&M mean?

    Time & Materials is a product development contract model when you pay the developer based upon the time and materials spent in order to achieve the results. In other words, the model has a “pay as you go” basis.

    T&M is good for the projects where you can’t determine the final amount of work or timing. Application development splits into tasks which are evaluated separately. The main advantage of the model is its agility. You can add changes to the project during the development process and estimate its results after each iteration.

  • I have an unfinished app. Can you complete it?

    We are ready to complete your application. We will need the original design, the source code, and the technical specification.

    We will review the documentation, design, and the code. It allows us to finalize the project status. You will receive a report and a T&M estimation in order to complete your project.
